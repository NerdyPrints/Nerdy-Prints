Privacy Policy

Nerdy Prints is an online store offering custom-designed fandom merchandise. Our website is https://nerdyprints.co.uk.

What Data We Collect

When you sign up or place an order, we collect the following data:

  • Full Name

We collect this data to:

Your data is securely stored in a Google Sheet accessed only by authorized Nerdy Prints personnel.

Who We Share Your Data With

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may share necessary details with our printing and shipping partners (e.g., Printful) solely to fulfill your orders.
